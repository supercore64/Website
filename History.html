Analysing Sources

  • What is the subject of the photo? What does it reveal about it's subject
  • What is the setting?
  • What other details?
  • When and where in the past do you think the photograph was taken? How can you tell?
  • How would you describe the photographer's point of view? (bias, perspective)
  • List objects, people or symbols in cartoon
  • What do you think the symbol means?
  • Describe the action taking place
  • Are there words? Do they help clarify the symbols?
  • In your opinion, what is the message? Will other's interpret it differently?
  • When and where was the map produced
  • What details has the map-maker chosen to include (or exclude) in order to complete this representation?
  • Why was the map drawn?
  • Why was the artefact created?
  • What and where would it have been used?
  • What does the artefact tell us about technology at the time of its creation?
  • What can it tell us about the life and times of the people who used it?
