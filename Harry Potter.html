What is a monologue?

A monologue is a little speech spoken in front of some people. It is a play that you do not act out, its more like a presentation speech. Instead of acting out how you feel in a performance on stage, this is a presentation that expresses how you feel and what your thinking on stage in front of a little audience.

How to start a monologue?

Start with a hook because a hook will keep the audience on the edge of their seat wanting more. If you don't start with a hook, the audience will become bored and not want to listen anymore.

Key steps to success when writing a monologue:

  1. Keep it short as possible
  2. Be Authentic
  3. Write an entertaining monologue
  4. Find a monologue that fits your every description
  5. Elements of surprise
  6. Mind your language
  7. Choose to be a winner in a monologue
  8. Get one with a storyline
